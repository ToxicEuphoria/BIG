COMPUTER SCIENCE FOR TROUBLED YOUTH

Our mobile tutors go directly to your child's boardingschool to provide high quality 1-on-1 computer science mentorship sessions.

A FOCUS ON GIRLS

Boarding schools often lack the specialized resources and talent required to teach computer science to youth. Our mobile tutors solve that problem through weekly or daily in-house visits made directly to your child's treatment center or mental care facility. There we provide 1-on-1 tutoring sessions to augment on top of your child's existing curriculum, designed to teach the key problem solving skills that computer science provides.

We are dedicated to growing computer programming education within boarding schools. Our vision is that every girl should have the opportunity to learn computer science.

We believe computer science should be part of the core curriculum in education, alongside other science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) courses, such as biology, physics, chemistry and algebra.

Troubled youth in particular are the most underepresented, yet we've found they have the most passion. Once exposed to computer science for the very first time they often realize that computer science can empower them to change their lives for the better.

In fact, it's not at all unusual for kids that coded as a hobby to land six figure salary jobs right out of highschool!

OUR PROCESS

Introduction

First we setup a conversation to answer any questions you may have about our service. This can be done eletronically via Skype, Google Hangout, or other method of your choosing. If you are also based in the Layton, Utah area we are happy to meet in person. 801-793-1020

The Approval

Because there is paper work and sensitive data involved this is not a 'click and done' sort of deal. We want to make sure we have clients that are worth our time and looking for a long term relationship. Note: Also you'll need to ask the school to put our mentor on their allowed visitor list. While some youth programs don't allow for visitors, after they learn about the good we are doing they almost always make an exception.

Payment

We offer 2 packages: Our standard package is $999 per month, and includes 1 Hour of tutoring time per day. The second package is our economy package, it is $499 per month and includes 30 Minutes of tutoring per day. We do our best to work with your child's school to schedule these times in advance. But we're very flexible. As needed we will roll absent sessions over to the following week.
